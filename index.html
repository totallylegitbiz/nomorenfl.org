no more nfl

Players abuse their spouses and family members with little recourse. League veterans suffer from concussions as a workplace hazard with few changes ahead. Cheerleaders don't even make minimum wage. To top it all, off, the NFL doesn't even pay taxes.

We've been football fans our whole lives, but 2015 is the year that we say that this is not okay. Until the NFL respect its players, their families, and the rest of our tax-paying country, #nomorenfl.

We dare you to boycott the NFL in 2015. Or, if you just can't quit it, open your wallet to one of these good causes.
